Hetula
(Hewan Tumbuhan Langka)

Android + Google Maps

An app that can provide information about rare animals and plants found in Indonesia. The user is given a picture of islands throughout Indonesia, to see rare animals / plants in an island, click on the picture of the island. Charts of rare animals and plants for each island are also displayed. Users can insert, update, and delete data. Users can also see the location of these animals / plants through the MAP feature.
